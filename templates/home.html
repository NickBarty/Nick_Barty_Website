HECS debt can be hard to understand, but it shouldn't be.
The following are facts about HECS debt:

  • HECS Debt is indexed annually on June 1
  • 'Indexation' is the same as interest on a loan, just different terminology
  • Only HECS debt you have had for more than 1 year has indexation applied to it
  • You only make automatic repayments on a HECS debt when your annual income is above a certain amount
  • In the vast majority of cases, making even a $10 weekly voluntary contribution towards your debt will save you years and thousands of dollars in the long run
